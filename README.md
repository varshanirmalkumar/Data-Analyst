## Project Overview

This project analyses a large-scale financial dataset spanning **3 years (2021–2023)**, **6 global regions**, and **€373M+ in total revenue**. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into a clean, interactive dashboard that enables business stakeholders to explore performance trends and make data-driven decisions — without needing to touch a spreadsheet.

---

## Dataset

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Records | 30,000 rows |
| Period | 2021 – 2023 |
| Regions | Benelux, EMEA, APAC, Americas, DACH, Nordics |
| Revenue categories | Product Sales, Subscriptions, Services |
| Total revenue | €373M+ |

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Scorecards
- Total Revenue
- Net Profit
- Average Gross Margin %

### Visualisations
- **Revenue & expense trends** — quarterly and annual trend lines
- **Regional breakdown** — side-by-side bar charts across 6 global regions
- **Category revenue mix** — treemap showing Product Sales vs Subscriptions vs Services
- **Interactive filters** — slice by Year, Quarter, and Region across all visuals

---

## What I Built Under the Hood

- **Calculated fields** for Net Profit and Gross Margin %
- **Linked filters** so every chart responds to the same Year / Quarter / Region selection
- **Treemap + trend line + bar chart** layout designed for executive-level readability
- Data cleaning and preparation prior to import
